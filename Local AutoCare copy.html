Our Services

We offer a range of car washing and detailing services to enhance the appearance and performance of your vehicle.

Total Cost: 0

Standard

The Standard service provides a quick and convenient way to keep your car clean. Here's how it benefits your vehicle:

  • Removes dirt and grime from the exterior.
  • Vacuums and cleans the interior, improving the overall look and feel.
  • Wheels and tires are cleaned.
  • Interior surfaces are wiped.
  • Helps maintain your car's value and appearance.

This wash is priced at $35.

Premium 👑

The Premium service is a comprehensive cleaning and valet for car enthusiasts who care deeply about their vehicle's well-being. Here's how it benefits your car:

  • Thorough vacuuming and carpet valet to remove dirt and debris.
  • Exterior wash and wax for a brilliant shine and paint protection.
  • Wheel cleaning and shining for a like-new appearance.
  • Interior surfaces are meticulously wiped and shined.
  • Fragrance treatment to leave your car smelling brand new.
  • Engine bay cleaning for a clean and organized engine compartment.
  • Exhaust pipe cleaning and shining for added visual appeal.
  • Boot cleaning, vacuuming, and wiping.
  • Window cleaning for clear visibility.
  • Enhances your car's aesthetics and resale value.

This wash is priced at $50.

Interior

The Interior service focuses on revitalizing your car's interior. Here's how it benefits your vehicle:

  • Thorough vacuuming and carpet valet to remove dirt and debris.
  • Interior surfaces are expertly wiped and shined for a clean and fresh look.
  • Fragrance treatment leaves your car smelling brand new.
  • Boot cleaning, vacuuming, and wiping.
  • Window cleaning for clear visibility.
  • Improves interior aesthetics and comfort.

This wash is priced at $30.

Exterior

The Exterior service is designed to enhance your car's exterior appearance and protection. Here's how it benefits your vehicle:

  • Thorough wash and wax to protect the paint and maintain its shine.
  • Wheel cleaning and shining for an impressive look.
  • Engine bay cleaning for a clean and organized engine compartment.
  • Exhaust pipe cleaning and shining for added visual appeal.
  • Preserves your car's exterior and extends its lifespan.

This wash is priced at $20.

Supreme

The supreme wash is a good wash for a mid-range between the premium and standard wash.

  • Removes dirt and grime from the exterior, protecting the paint, and giving a clean shine.
  • Vacuums and cleans the interior, improving the overall look and feel.
  • Wheels and tires are cleaned and shined for a polished appearance.
  • Interior surfaces are wiped and shined for a fresh, interior with a polish to make it shine.
  • Provides a ultra glossy and clean car.

This wash is priced at $40.
